## Frequently Asked Questions

**H2:** Frequently Asked Questions — Prime Electrical Auckland

**Q: What electrical services does Prime Electrical offer in Auckland?**
Prime Electrical provides a full range of residential and commercial electrical services in Auckland, including general electrical repairs and installations, electrical rewiring, indoor and outdoor lighting, heat pump installation, solar panel systems, smart home automation, and 24/7 emergency electrical response across the Auckland region.

**Q: Is Prime Electrical a licensed electrician in Auckland?**
Yes. Prime Electrical is a licensed and registered electrical company based in East Tāmaki, Auckland. All electricians are qualified, trained, and certified, ensuring every job is completed safely and to New Zealand's electrical standards. Prime Electrical has been operating since 2011.

**Q: Does Prime Electrical install heat pumps in Auckland?**
Yes. Prime Electrical is an authorised installer for Mitsubishi, Daikin, Panasonic, Carrier, and Toshiba heat pumps across Auckland and Hamilton. We install both split systems and ducted systems for residential and commercial properties, and offer free consultations.

**Q: Does Prime Electrical install solar panels in Auckland?**
Yes. Prime Electrical installs solar panel systems across Auckland and Hamilton. All solar panels come with a minimum 25-year performance warranty. We are Clean Energy Council-accredited and manage the full process including paperwork, installation, and grid connection.

**Q: Does Prime Electrical offer smart home automation in Auckland?**
Yes. Prime Electrical sets up smart home systems in Auckland, including automated lighting, heating control, security, and voice-activated home management. Our wireless systems require no additional wiring and are customised to suit your home's layout and preferences.

**Q: Does Prime Electrical offer 24/7 emergency electrical services?**
Yes. Prime Electrical provides 24/7 emergency electrical response across Auckland. If you have an urgent electrical fault, call 09-390-3620 and our team will attend promptly to diagnose and resolve the issue safely.

**Q: Where is Prime Electrical located, and what areas do they cover?**
Prime Electrical is based at Unit 2, 41 Smales Road, East Tāmaki, Auckland. We service East Auckland, South Auckland (Manukau, Franklin), Auckland Central, North Shore, West Auckland, Rodney, and Hamilton.

**Q: How do I get a free quote from Prime Electrical?**
Call 09-390-3620, email sales@theprimeelectrical.co.nz, or fill in the free quote form on the website. Prime Electrical offers free estimates for all services — electrical, heat pump, solar, and smart home automation — with a response within 12–72 hours.
