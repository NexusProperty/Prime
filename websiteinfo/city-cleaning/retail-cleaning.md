## Overview

Creating Clean, Welcoming Retail Spaces.

In retail, presentation is everything. A spotless store builds trust, improves customer experience, and reflects the quality of your brand.

City Cleaning helps retailers, shopping centres, and hospitality venues maintain clean, safe, and inviting environments every day. From boutique stores to large-scale retail complexes.

## Cleaning That Fits Your Trading Hours

Teams work early mornings, late nights, or during quiet trading periods. Regular cleaning, floor polishing, hygiene servicing. Rapid-response cleaning for food courts, restrooms, and high-traffic areas. Health regulations compliant.
