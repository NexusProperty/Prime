## Overview

At City Cleaning, we provide a complete range of commercial cleaning and hygiene solutions designed for workplaces across New Zealand.

From high-use government buildings and public facilities to corporate offices, schools, and industrial sites, our services are tailored to meet your site's needs, schedule, and standards. Our focus is on reliability, sustainability, and measurable results delivered by people who care about the details.

---

## Tailored Cleaning & Hygiene Solutions

No two sites are the same. All cleaning is delivered using best-practice systems, eco-certified Multi-Clean green seal certified products, and sustainable consumables such as Tork tissue.

### Commercial Cleaning

Reliable, high-quality cleaning for workplaces of every size. Flexible schedules that fit the way you work.

### Day Cleaning & Hostess Duties

Day cleaners and hostesses maintain your workplace throughout business hours. They manage restrooms, kitchens, and shared areas quietly in the background.

### Hygiene Services

Comprehensive hygiene servicing delivered directly by City Cleaning staff. Sanitary and nappy bins, soap, air fresheners. Sustainable, non-aerosol systems.

### Planned Periodic Cleaning

Daily maintenance combined with planned deep cleans. Every programme tailored to your site's layout, usage, and hours.

### Consumables

Full range of cleaning and hygiene consumables. Partners: Tork, Eco Bags, Ecostore.

### Carpet & Upholstery

Deep cleaning using eco-friendly products and low-moisture systems. Hot-water extraction and steam cleaning.

### Interior & Exterior Window Cleaning

Professional window cleaning for a streak-free finish. Purified water systems, rope access, elevated platforms.

### Exterior Building Washing

Gentle, sustainable exterior cleaning. Soft-wash and water-efficient methods.

### Rubbish Removal & Waste Auditing

Daily rubbish removal with detailed auditing. Recycling, composting, landfill reduction programmes.

### Floor Restoration & Hard Floor Care

Expert restoration and maintenance for all types of hard flooring. Strip, seal, polish, burnish.
