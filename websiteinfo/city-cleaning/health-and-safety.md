## Overview

Safe People, Safe Workplaces.

At City Cleaning, safety comes first — always. It's built into everything we do. Our goal: to keep everyone safe — our staff, your staff, and anyone who enters the spaces we clean.

---

## Certified Systems and Standards

Health and safety systems meet ISO48001 standards through Tōtika. Align with the Health and Safety at Work Act 2015.

SiteWise Green and Impac PreQual 5-Star accreditations — independent verification that our policies, training, and procedures meet New Zealand's highest benchmarks.

---

## Training and Awareness

Every team member receives full induction and site-specific training before starting work.

**Instruction in:**
- Hazard identification and control
- Safe chemical handling
- PPE use and maintenance
- Manual handling
- Incident and near-miss reporting
- Emergency procedures

Regular refresher sessions and toolbox talks. Supervisors complete ongoing leadership and first aid training.

---

## In-House Electrical Testing & Maintenance

City Cleaning employs its own qualified electrician to Test & Tag all equipment nationwide. Detailed compliance calendar. Audits all cleaning equipment. Internal service for tighter control, faster response, consistent performance.

---

## Real-Time Reporting and Visibility

Digital cloud-based technology records and tracks all site incidents, inspections, and safety audits in real time. Prompt reporting, accurate documentation, clear accountability. Mobile check-ins, corrective action logs. Compliance demonstrable at any time.

---

## A Culture of Care

Safety isn't just a checklist — it's part of our culture. Open communication. Every team member empowered to speak up about risks, suggest improvements, or ask for help. We recognise and celebrate safe behaviour.
