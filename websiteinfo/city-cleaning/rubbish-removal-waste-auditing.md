## Overview

Smarter Waste Solutions for Cleaner, Greener Workplaces.

Waste management isn't just about emptying bins — it's about creating cleaner, more efficient, and more sustainable workplaces. City Cleaning's rubbish removal and waste auditing services help you reduce landfill, improve recycling, and manage waste responsibly across all your sites.

---

## Everyday Waste Management

We manage general waste, recycling, and compostable materials. All bins emptied, cleaned, and relined as part of your cleaning routine.

**Waste streams:**
- General waste
- Mixed recycling
- Paper and cardboard
- Food and organic waste
- E-waste or site-specific collections

---

## Data-Driven Waste Auditing

Tracks how much waste your site produces, where it's going, and how it can be reduced. Digital reporting and site inspections. Clear reports for savings and sustainability gains.

**Auditing helps you:**
- Monitor waste volumes by stream
- Track recycling and landfill diversion rates
- Demonstrate sustainability performance for ESG reporting
- Set achievable reduction goals backed by real data
