## Overview

Cleaning That Cares for the Planet.

Sustainability isn't an add-on at City Cleaning — it's part of who we are. Clean workplaces shouldn't come at the cost of the environment. Every decision we make is guided by environmental responsibility.

---

## Toitū Enviromark Diamond Certified

The highest level of environmental management certification available in New Zealand. Independent certification confirms our environmental systems meet the ISO 14001 equivalent standard, with continuous improvement and verified performance.

Our environmental management system covers product selection, waste reduction, staff training, energy use.

---

## Eco-Friendly Products and Practices

Green Earth-certified cleaning products — biodegradable, non-toxic, safe for people and the environment. Trusted sustainable brands: Tork, Ecostore, Eco Bags.

Teams trained to use correct dosages and techniques to minimise chemical and water waste while maintaining exceptional hygiene standards.

---

## Reducing Waste and Emissions

- Route planning to minimise travel and fuel use
- Biodegradable bin liners and recyclable packaging
- Digital reporting to eliminate paper use
- Recycling and composting programmes at major sites

---

## Sustainable Partnerships

We work with suppliers who share our commitment to sustainability — responsible sourcing, ethical labour practices, environmental performance. We help clients achieve ESG and sustainability targets.

---

## A Long-Term Commitment

Sustainability isn't a one-time project — it's a mindset. Always looking for new ways to improve: low-emission vehicles, expanding recycling initiatives, testing innovative eco-products.
