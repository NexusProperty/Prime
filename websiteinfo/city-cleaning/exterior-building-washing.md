## Overview

Exterior Cleaning That Protects and Preserves Your Property.

The outside of your building is the first thing people see. Over time, weather, pollution, and organic growth can make even the best-kept facilities look tired. City Cleaning's building washing services restore your exterior surfaces, protecting your investment and helping your property look its best year-round.

Trained technicians use safe, proven methods to remove dirt, mould, mildew, and stains from all building types.

---

## Gentle on Surfaces, Tough on Grime

Low-pressure washing, soft-wash techniques, environmentally friendly cleaning agents. We assess surface condition before selecting the most effective and safe cleaning process. Brick, concrete, metal, painted weatherboard.

---

## Improving Presentation and Protection

- Prevent mould and lichen build-up
- Maintain paint and surface coatings
- Reduce wear from salt and pollution
- Improve safety by removing slippery residues on walkways and entries

---

## Safe Work Practices

Fully trained and certified in working at heights, harness use, elevated work platforms. Comprehensive site risk assessments. Health and Safety at Work Act 2015 compliant. Containment methods to prevent run-off from entering stormwater drains.
