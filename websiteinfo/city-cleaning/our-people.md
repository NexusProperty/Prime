## Overview

The Heart of City Cleaning.

Behind every clean workplace are the people who make it happen. Our 700-plus staff are the heart of City Cleaning — a team of dedicated individuals who take pride in doing the job right, every time.

From Site Cleaners and Supervisors to Account Managers and Operations Leaders. Every person receives site-specific training — health and safety, chemical handling, infection control, security procedures. Regular refreshers, leadership development, performance reviews.

---

## A Team That Reflects New Zealand

Proudly multicultural workforce. Pacific, Māori, Indian, Asian, African, and European backgrounds. Diversity strengthens who we are and how we serve. Respect, fairness, and inclusion.
