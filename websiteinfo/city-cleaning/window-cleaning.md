## Overview

Crystal-Clear Results, Inside and Out.

Clean windows don't just brighten a space — they reflect your organisation's standards. City Cleaning's interior and exterior window cleaning services keep your buildings looking their best, inside and out.

Trained technicians deliver streak-free, high-quality results using safe, sustainable methods suited to every environment from ground-level offices to high-rise glass façades.

## Exterior & Interior Window Cleaning

Complete interior and exterior window cleaning for all types of buildings. Using water-fed poles, rope-access systems, and elevated work platforms. For interiors: dust, fingerprints, smudges using lint-free cloths and eco-friendly products. Glass partitions, skylights, mirrors, feature glazing.
