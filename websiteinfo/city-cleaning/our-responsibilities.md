## Overview

Doing What's Right for People, Communities, and the Planet.

At City Cleaning, responsibility doesn't stop at providing great cleaning — it extends to how we treat people, how we operate, and how we impact the world around us.

Business success should create positive outcomes: fair employment, ethical partnerships, safe workplaces, genuine contribution to the communities we're part of.

---

## Supporting Our People

Stable employment, fair pay, equal opportunities for over 700 staff across New Zealand. We're one of the few cleaning companies that pay our team weekly — helps them manage finances with confidence and stability.

Training and development built into every role. We prioritise wellbeing and respect. We celebrate the diversity of our workforce — multicultural, inclusive environment where everyone belongs.

---

## Strengthening Local Communities

New Zealand–owned company. We employ locally, buy locally, partner with NZ suppliers. We support local schools, community projects, national charities.

**Nurturing Families** — charity providing essential resources and support to whānau in need. Founded by Tayla Nasmith at 12 years old. Helps hundreds of New Zealand families each month.

## Ethical and Transparent Business Practices

Integrity is the foundation. Transparency and honesty in everything we do. Policies and procedures ensure fair, compliant, ethical operations. Open communication with clients, employees, partners.

Accreditations: Toitū Enviromark Diamond, Toitika, SiteWise Green, Impac PreQual 5-Star — verified performance standards we live by.

## Environmental Responsibility

Certified Environmental Management System. Reduce waste, lower emissions, use sustainable products. Help clients reach environmental and ESG goals.
