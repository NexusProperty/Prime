## Overview

Join a Team That Values You.

At City Cleaning, our people are at the heart of everything we do. Whether you're starting out or bringing years of experience, we offer secure, meaningful work that makes a difference every day.

We're always looking for reliable, motivated people who take pride in doing a great job. Trusted by some of New Zealand's leading organisations — schools, hospitals, government, corporate offices.

---

## Why Work With City Cleaning?

- **Fair pay and stable hours** — reliable employment with weekly pay and consistent rosters
- **Training and support** — full site-specific training and ongoing upskilling opportunities
- **Career growth** — many supervisors and managers started as cleaners
- **Respect and inclusion** — team represents cultures from across Aotearoa and the world
- **Safety first** — all roles follow certified health and safety procedures

---

## What We Look For

- Take pride in their work and pay attention to detail
- Dependable and punctual
- Work well as part of a team
- Care about health, safety, and doing the job right

Previous cleaning experience is great but not essential — we'll give you the training you need. If you bring the right attitude, we'll provide the rest.

---

## Our Commitment to Our People

Fair treatment, open communication, celebrating good work. HR and management teams always available — extra training, wellbeing assistance, help balancing work and family life. Opportunities for people from all walks of life. Long-term careers.
