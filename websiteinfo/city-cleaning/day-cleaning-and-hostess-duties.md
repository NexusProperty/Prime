## Overview

Keeping Your Workplace Immaculate, All Day Long.

When your site is open throughout the day, cleaning can't just happen after hours. City Cleaning's day cleaning and hostess services keep your spaces clean, stocked, and welcoming from morning to close without disrupting the flow of your business.

---

## A Visible Difference — Without the Disruption

Day cleaning teams work efficiently and respectfully around your staff and visitors. They blend into your workplace, keeping high-traffic areas clean while maintaining professionalism and discretion.

Tasks: emptying bins, loading dishwashers, wiping surfaces, restocking consumables, stationery services, handling quick spills. Hostess duties for larger or multi-tenant sites — washrooms, reception areas, shared facilities.

---

## The Benefits of Day Cleaning

- Continuous cleanliness throughout working hours
- Faster response to spills, breakages, and high-use areas
- Reduced after-hours energy use and security costs
- Improved staff and visitor experience
- Real-time oversight and accountability

Ideal for: government offices, educational facilities, transport hubs, retail environments.

## Tailored to Your Site and Schedule

Every day cleaning plan is individually tailored. Account Manager regularly reviews performance and adapts routines as your site's needs change.

## A Team You'll Know by Name

Day cleaners become a trusted part of your workplace culture. Each team member is trained in City Cleaning's exacting standards, with site-specific inductions and ongoing coaching in health, safety, and hygiene protocols.
