## Overview

Trusted Cleaning for Government and Public Facilities.

City Cleaning has built a long-standing reputation as a trusted partner for Government departments, local councils, and public organisations across New Zealand.

Public sector facilities require reliability, security, and professionalism. Whether it's an office, service centre, courthouse, library, or community hub, our teams deliver high-quality cleaning that supports the integrity and safety of these essential environments.
