## Company Info

**H2:** About Us

> Paramount has provided commercial cleaning services to businesses, councils and government agencies for 45 years. As one of the most established cleaning companies in New Zealand, we operate through a network of owner-operated cleaning franchises, delivering full-service regular cleaning programmes and one-off jobs like carpet cleaning. We have a workforce of 1,600 servicing over 5,100 locations across New Zealand. Our franchise network currently consists of more than 220 franchisees. Alongside full domestic cleaning services, our cleaning business focuses on green eco-friendly cleaning services, helping your business to care better for staff, customers and the environment.
