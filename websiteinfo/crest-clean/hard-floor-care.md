## Overview

We love hard floors; it's where we really shine. We have trained, experienced personnel in each region who are skilled at maintaining hard floors.

Polishing and restoring the shine to vinyl floors and other hard floor surfaces is a specialist trade performed by CrestClean's trained and experienced personnel. We use the latest equipment and high-quality polishes to restore a gloss shine to hard floor surfaces.

Our hard floor care maintenance plans keep your floor in great condition by scheduling periodic buffing and polishing, to save you money over time and keep your premises looking professional.
