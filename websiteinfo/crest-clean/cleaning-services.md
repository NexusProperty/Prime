## Overview

CrestClean provides a full range of cleaning services for businesses, schools and organisations across New Zealand.

We have professionally trained cleaning teams, ready to shine. CrestClean provides consistent and reliable cleaning services to more than 6000 customers throughout the country.

Our cleaning teams are owner-operators – they have a vested interest in providing high standards of cleaning. They receive professional training and regular skills development to ensure they deliver a quality service.

Our Toitū Enviromark Diamond certification helps to improve the sustainability of our cleaning services and allows our customers to verify sustainable procurement.

[Contact your local CrestClean Regional Manager today](https://crestclean.co.nz/contact-us/)— we are ready and able to make a real difference to your workplace cleaning.

### CrestClean offers:

- Regional Managers who provide a single point of contact
- Security vetted personnel in uniforms with photo IDs
- Scheduled quality audits
- Detailed specifications for your workplace cleaning
- Professionally trained cleaning teams via our Master Cleaners Training Institute programmes
- A stable and secure workforce of owner-operators

## Our Services

### Contract Commercial Cleaning

Your local CrestClean teams are experts at contract cleaning. Our Regional Managers provide your single point of contact. They are responsible for assuring the quality and reliability of our cleaning services. Cleaning is carried out in strict accordance with the 'Schedule of Duties' that is provided with every quote and approved by you prior to the contract commencement. Cleaning service times are agreed in advance and the itemised schedule ensures you get exactly what you need.

[Learn more](https://crestclean.co.nz/cleaning-services/contract-cleaning/)

### Healthcare Cleaning

CrestClean Healthcare delivers specialist cleaning services tailored to New Zealand's medical and clinical environments. With decades of experience and nationwide coverage, we understand the specific needs of the healthcare sector. Our HealthClean® system ensures consistent, hygienic outcomes across private hospitals, surgeries, and clinics.

[Learn more](https://crestclean.co.nz/cleaning-services/healthcare/)

### Carpet & Upholstery Cleaning

CrestClean's qualified and experienced carpet cleaning specialists extend the life of your carpets and improve the health and hygiene in your workplace. Our Carpet Care operators attend specialised training courses to make them experts in carpet cleaning and stain removal. They use the latest portable hot water extraction equipment to guarantee a great result.

[Learn more](https://crestclean.co.nz/cleaning-services/commercial-carpet-cleaning/)

### Hard Floor Cleaning and Maintenance

Polishing and restoring your hard floor surfaces is a specialised service. You can count on CrestClean's well-trained operators to bring back the shine. We use the latest high-tech polishing pads and professional strength polishes to restore a gloss shine to all types of hard floors.

[Learn more](https://crestclean.co.nz/cleaning-services/hard-floor-care/)

### Pure Water Window Cleaning

CrestClean's Pure Water Window Cleaning service provides cost-effective window cleaning for buildings up to four storeys high. No chemicals are used in Pure Water Window Cleaning, which is both economical and environmentally responsible. With the latest carbon fibre extendable poles we will clean windows up to four storeys high without using lifting platforms or ladders, making Pure Water a safer alternative to other commercial window cleaning operations.

[Learn more](https://crestclean.co.nz/cleaning-services/window-cleaning/)

### Hygiene Consumables and Services

Through our partnership with Alsco Uniforms and Fresh & Clean we offer an extensive range of consumables, hygiene solutions, and rental equipment with an efficient pick-up and delivery service. We can also arrange hassle-free installation of new hand towel and soap dispensers – often free of charge!

[Learn more](https://crestclean.co.nz/cleaning-services/consumables/)

### Property Caretaking

Crest PropertyCare provides reliable property care services for businesses, organisations and schools. Commercial buildings and business premises need regular exterior maintenance to improve their presentation. Whether it's spraying for moss or sweeping up leaves and windblown rubbish, our property care teams take pride in their work and can help maintain your premises and grounds to a high standard.

[Learn more](https://crestclean.co.nz/cleaning-services/caretaking/)

### Sanitising Service

Sanitising high-touch surfaces improves workplace hygiene. We use a 2-step process where surfaces are first cleaned to remove soil and biofilm build-up, and then sanitiser is applied. The service is carried out at agreed frequencies i.e. daily, 3 x weekly, or weekly, in addition to our standard cleaning services.

[Learn more](https://crestclean.co.nz/cleaning-services/contract-cleaning/sanitising-service/)
