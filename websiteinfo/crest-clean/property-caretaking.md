## Appraisal and quotation process

We provide a consultative quotation – all property care tasks are defined and ordered into a schedule of duties, with regular, seasonal and periodic tasks. The schedule of duties forms the basis for an annual service on a contract arrangement.

We deploy 'Safe Systems of Work' for each service that our personnel carry out.

All personnel are background checked and security vetted.
