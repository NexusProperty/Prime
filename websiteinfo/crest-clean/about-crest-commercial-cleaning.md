## Overview

New Zealand owned and locally owned and operated in each city, Crest Commercial Cleaning Ltd has been serving happy customers since 1996.

We currently have a team of over 2300 personnel who provide cleaning services to more than 6,300 customers nationwide.

Crest Commercial Cleaning Ltd has built a solid reputation for its comprehensive support systems. With centralised training, information systems, marketing and quality assurance programmes, CrestClean personnel are well supported and dedicated to providing complete customer satisfaction.

Where most cleaning companies just use elbow grease, our cleaners are trained to use their heads. The qualifications required by CrestClean sets 'best practice' for the entire commercial cleaning industry in New Zealand.

---

## Training & Qualifications

CrestClean is the largest trainer of cleaning personnel in NZ. Each year we host more than 200 training events for our personnel nationwide. Our personnel are trained by the Master Cleaners Training Institute, which provides practical cleaning training, proficiency assessments and annual upskilling.

More than 500 CrestClean cleaners have gained the British Institute of Cleaning Science Professional Skills Suite Certificate. This course was validated by an NZQA accredited Industry Training Organisation that moderated our course content.

Training programmes meet internationally recognised industry training standards in practical cleaning techniques and Health and Safety.

## The Team at CrestClean

CrestClean's personnel are franchised owner operators who physically carry out the cleaning of their customers' premises. They have invested in their own local business and they are committed to providing good customer service because they are working for themselves.

Our Regional Master Franchisees lead CrestClean's operations in their local areas. They have sound industry knowledge and provide a solutions-based approach to managing the customer relationship. Communication is the most important part of account management.

CrestClean Admin Services is our national support office that provides corporate services to customers and franchisees. All invoicing and contracts administration is centralised. We operate Microsoft Dynamics GP and Microsoft CRM as a leading edge IT platform.

With 20 years of interfacing with customers, ranging from large corporates to small businesses, CrestClean has gained an excellent reputation for our accurate invoicing and professionalism in managing long-term customer relationships.
