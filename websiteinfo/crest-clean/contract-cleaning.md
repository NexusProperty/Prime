## How It Works

> Our Regional Master Franchisees oversee the quality and reliability of our cleaning service, and provide a direct point of contact.
> Your local CrestClean team are experts at contract cleaning. Our Regional Master Franchisees are your direct point of contact. They are responsible for assuring the quality and reliability of our cleaning service.

**Key points:**
- Cleaning is carried out in strict accordance with the 'Schedule of Duties' provided with every quote and approved by you prior to contract commencement
- Cleaning service times are agreed in advance; itemised schedule ensures you get exactly what you need
- Every site has a dedicated Communication Book for "day-to-day" messages and to log additional requests
- For large-scale premises we create site-specific Quality Audit documentation
