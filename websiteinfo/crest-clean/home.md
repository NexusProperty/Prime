## Commercial Cleaning Overview

**H1:** Commercial Cleaning

> Since our establishment in 1996, CrestClean has become a leader in commercial cleaning and sanitising.
> We have earned a strong reputation for innovation and leadership, and excellence in quality, safety, and customer service. Our dedicated team of over 2,200 personnel serves more than 6,000 customers nationwide, spanning the healthcare, office, manufacturing and industrial, education, and retail sectors.

**CrestClean delivers:**
- Regional Managers who provide a single point of contact
- Security vetted personnel in uniforms with photo IDs
- Scheduled quality audits
- Detailed specifications for your workplace cleaning
- Professionally trained cleaning teams via our Master Cleaners Training Institute programmes

**Certifications:** Toitū Enviromark Diamond certification for environmental sustainability
