## Overview

Crest Commercial Cleaning Limited (CrestClean, we, us, our) is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of any Personal Information you provide to us.

This Privacy Policy (Policy) applies to CrestClean's collection and use of Personal Information, being any information about an identifiable individual including personal information about a sole trader or a commercial customer's directors and/or other staff that engage with CrestClean on behalf of a customer (Personal Information).

Unless we expressly agree otherwise, any Personal Information you provide to us, whether via the CrestClean Customer Website (crestclean.co.nz) or otherwise, will be processed by us in accordance with this policy and all applicable privacy laws, including the Privacy Act 2020 (Privacy Act).

---

## Key Sections

- **Collection** – Information you provide directly, information collected automatically (cookies), information from third parties
- **Use** – Operating the Website, providing services, marketing (opt-in), customer support, transactions, compliance
- **Sharing** – Related companies, third-party service providers, regulators/law enforcement where required by law. CrestClean does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties.
- **Protection** – Reasonable safeguards against loss, unauthorised use, modification or disclosure
- **Your rights** – Access and correction rights under the Privacy Act. Contact Privacy Officer if you wish to exercise these rights.
