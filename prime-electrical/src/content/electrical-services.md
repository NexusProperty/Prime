### What Is Involved in Electrical Rewiring in Auckland?

Faulty or outdated electrical wiring is one of the most serious safety hazards in a home or commercial building. Prime Electrical's licensed rewiring team safely replaces old or damaged wiring, addressing ground faults, short circuits, and other dangerous electrical conditions before they cause fires or injury.

**Signs your Auckland property may need rewiring:**
- Frequently tripping circuit breakers
- Flickering or dimming lights
- Burning smell or scorch marks near power points
- Electrical wiring more than 25 years old
- Planning a major renovation that requires updated electrical capacity

Call 09-390-3620 for a free assessment and quote on electrical rewiring in Auckland.
