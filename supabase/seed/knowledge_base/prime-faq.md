## Services

### What electrical services do you offer?
We offer a full range of residential and commercial electrical services including new wiring and rewiring, heat pump supply and installation, EV charger installation, solar panel system connection, LED lighting upgrades, switchboard upgrades and replacements, smart home and automation systems, and general electrical repairs and maintenance.

### Do you do heat pump installations?
Yes. We supply and install leading brands including Mitsubishi, Fujitsu, Daikin, and Panasonic. A single heat pump supply and install typically starts from $2,800 including labour and GST. Multi-head systems start from $5,500. All installs include a 5-year manufacturer warranty and our workmanship guarantee.

### Can you install an EV charger?
Yes. We are certified EV charger installers. A single-phase home charger install typically starts from $1,200 including the charger unit. Three-phase fast chargers for commercial use start from $2,500. We can assess your switchboard capacity during a free site visit.

### Do you offer solar panel installation?
We handle the electrical connection and inverter wiring for solar PV systems. We work alongside your solar panel supplier. The electrical component typically starts from $1,500 depending on system size.

### What about switchboard upgrades?
Switchboard upgrades to modern safety switch (RCD) compliant boards start from $1,800 for a standard residential property. This includes new circuit breakers and safety switches meeting current NZ standards.

### Do you do LED lighting upgrades?
Yes. Replacing halogen downlights with LED is one of our most popular jobs. LED retrofit downlights start from $25 per light installed. Whole-house packages available with volume pricing.

## Pricing

### How much does electrical work cost in NZ?
All electrical work is quoted per job. Rough guide: call-out and first hour from $180, additional labour from $120 per hour. Small jobs like adding a power point typically start from $250. All prices exclude GST.

### Do you offer free quotes?
Yes. We offer free quotes for jobs over $500. For smaller jobs we can give an estimate over the phone.

### Do you offer financing?
Yes. We offer Q Mastercard and GEM Visa financing options. Ask our team about 0% interest periods on purchases over $1,000. Subject to approval.

### Where do you operate?

## Emergency

### Do you offer emergency callouts?
Yes, 24/7 emergency electrical callouts are available. Emergency call-out rates apply after hours. Call 0800 PRIME 24 for emergencies. If there is immediate danger (fire, flooding near electrics), call 111 first.

## Licensing

### Are you licensed?
Yes. All our electricians hold current Electrical Worker licences issued by the Electrical Workers Registration Board (EWRB). All work is done to comply with New Zealand Electrical Code of Practice (NZECP).
